Our Story

Founded in 2024 by aerospace engineers and AI researchers, Aeronix was born from a simple observation: traditional inspection methods were too slow, expensive, and dangerous.

We set out to transform how industries collect aerial data. Starting with a single drone and two pilots, we've grown to a fleet of 25+ aircraft serving clients across 15 industries.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of the aerial robotics revolution, helping businesses make smarter decisions with precision data from above.
